We are very glad to welcome you here on the Health Bank web site. Let us start with a short tutorial.


  • On the top right of the screen you will find the action box:
    This box allows you to search your data, once you added something. At the end of your work here, you can log out from there. The more there are direct links to your profile and messages.
  • On the left you will find the navigation. Spaces is where your data is illustrated. Circles is for connecting to other users. Inbox is to send messages to others. In the apps section you will find the market with applications to add data to your account. And in the profile view, you can change your personal settings.
  • On this home site, you will find the latest news regarding Health Bank as well as a list of your latest data entries in the system (You will see content, as soon as you enter data).
  • We hope this helped you to get started and that you will enjoy your stay on the Health Bank platform.
