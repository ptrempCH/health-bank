Welcome to the profile page. Here you can see, edit and share your profile.


  • All the information we know about you is shown here. If you like to change something, click on the edit button at the bottom and fill out the form. All fields with a (*) are mendatory.
  • If you like to change your password, click on the change password button and provide a new one. As soon as you hit save, the new password is in place.
  • You can also change your user icon. To do so click on the corresponding button. There provide a PNG file and send it to us. Please keep in mind that your name and icon are visible for all users, also for those you have not in your circles.
  • If you like to share your profile with some circles, click on the circles button on the top right. Users you have in this circle will then see your address and other data. Everyone in a circle you did not select will only see your name and icon.
